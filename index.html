WHO I AM

Hello my friends! My name is Lewis Mathenge, and I am 23 years of age as of 2026.

Background History

I was born on 22nd April, 2003 in Embakasi, Nairobi, Kenya to parents Daniel Kooi and Lucy Wambui.

Education History

2008–2018: Primary Education: HillTop Academy (2008–2013), Sharp Education Centre (2014–2018).

2019–2022: Secondary Education: Giakanja Boys High School, Nyeri, Kenya.

2023–2024: Tertiary Education: Mount Kenya University (MKU), Thika, Kenya.

My Upbringing
  • I grew up in the slums of Kayole and had to 'survive for the fittest'.
  • I lived in Nairobi for 15 years until our relocation to Thika.
  • In May 2021, we relocated to Thika to escape rising living standards in Nairobi.
Tribe/Ethnic Roots

I am Kikuyu, with roots from Embu (Aembu society).
